Andy Rubin

Biography

Andy Rubin is an American computer programmer, entrepreneur, and venture capitalist. He is best known as the co-founder and former CEO of Android Inc., which was acquired by Google in 2005. Rubin's work has significantly shaped the mobile technology landscape we know today.
